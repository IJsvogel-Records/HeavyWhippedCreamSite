HEAVY WHIPPED CREAM is a blues rock/folk rock band from the Dutch heartland. Our bluesy, laid back stoner vibe is a straight descendant of the folky, rocky, sexy sixties sound of the soulful outsiders from Woodstock. Our musical journey is warmed by a 1970s glow, stirring the dusty road as, whilst jamming along, we head for unknown pastures and musical refuge from 'the man'.
