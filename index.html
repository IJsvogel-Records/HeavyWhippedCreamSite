HEAVY WHIPPED CREAM blends folk rock, indie and bluesy 60’s vibes – served with a psychedelic twist. Founded in the city of Utrecht in cold January evenings, the band released their debut EP ‘Cooking with…’ when summer reared its sunny head. Ever since, the band has been touring extensively, bringing their jamming, melodic tunes to theatres, cellars, pubs and caverns. A live EP and a summery single followed suit – and HEAVY WHIPPED CREAM continues cruising down the road on full gas.
